The Hidden Cost

Disorganized post production turns media into a long-term liability.

Without a plan, every new shoot adds complexity, cost, and chaos. Physical drives sprawl, cloud storage bills creep, links expire, archives become hard to retrieve, and the production slowly turns into a media hoarding facility.

Physical Hoarding

Out of sight, out of control

  • Drives everywhere, impossible to track
  • No visibility into what is important
  • High risk of loss, damage, or obsolescence
  • Wastes physical space and time

Cloud Cost Creep

Easy to start, expensive to ignore

  • More shoots equals more data and higher monthly bills
  • Unused, duplicated, and forgotten data stays online
  • No lifecycle policy means storage bloat
  • Egress fees can hit hard at delivery time
