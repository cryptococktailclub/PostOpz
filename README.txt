PostOpz hero fix package

1. Replace your current styles.css with this styles.css.
2. Upload assets/postopz-pz-icon-transparent.png into your repo's assets folder.
3. In index.html, point the hero logo image to:
   assets/postopz-pz-icon-transparent.png

Example:
<img src="assets/postopz-pz-icon-transparent.png" alt="PostOpz PZ logo">

This removes the white halo/background and scales the hero so the headline and icon do not crop.
